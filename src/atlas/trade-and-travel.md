# Trade and Travel

Emberwreath is interconnected, but it is not unified. Islands depend on one another, argue with one another, and send people across Emberwreath for practical reasons.

People move through trade, pilgrimage, seasonal work, convoy duty, water supply, ship repair, fishing, pilotage, medicine, timber, fiber, salt, and food. Travel is common enough for adventurers to exist, but dangerous enough to matter.

Breakwater is a major trade and repair hub, not the ruler of Emberwreath. It is a place where routes meet, ships are fixed, cargo changes hands, and many kinds of people pass through.

For characters shaped by more than one island, **Rainwall**, **Reedmere**, **Stormhook**, **Redwind**, and **Breakwater** are especially useful. They naturally connect people to water supply, fiber and sailcloth, fishing and rescue, dryland roads and salt, or island-spanning trade.

The Caldera and forbidden routes remain warnings, not casual shortcuts. A character can be shaped by fear, loss, temptation, taboo, or sailor stories without knowing the full truth behind them.
