# What Is Emberwreath?

Emberwreath is a volcanic archipelago. Sailors often call it **the Wreath**, while older poems and religious traditions sometimes call it **the Broken Wreath**.

No single crown, temple, guild, species, or island rules all of Emberwreath. The archipelago is connected by need, habit, trade, pilgrimage, old obligations, and sea routes rather than one central authority.

## A connected archipelago

The nine playable species began mostly on separate homeland islands:

- Humans from Breadbasket
- Zivari from Saffra
- Veyr from Sunspire
- Ferren from Greenwood
- Aumari from Tidefall Atolls
- Axali from Hollowdeep
- Scolen from Caelmere
- Kelden from Ashspine
- Thryss from Veilgarden

Contact between islands began around 500 years ago through scattered crossings, storms, trade, rescue, curiosity, and survival. Since then, the islands have become economically and culturally connected, even though old island traditions remain important.

## The danger at the center

The Caldera remains a central danger. The most recent major eruption happened about 60 years ago, close enough that elders, families, ports, temples, routes, and institutions still carry its consequences.

The world has recovered, but its systems remain brittle. Food, water, timber, fiber, medicine, safe routes, reliable pilots, and working ports all matter.
