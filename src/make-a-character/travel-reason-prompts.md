# Travel Reason Prompts

Choose one reason your character leaves home, or combine two that point in the same direction.

## Trade obligation

Who needs you to carry goods, news, payments, contracts, samples, or promises across Emberwreath?

## Family duty

Who in your family needs help, escort, repayment, representation, medicine, testimony, or news from another island?

## Pilgrimage

What vow, offering, memory, shrine, sacred water, or warning sends you onto the road or sea?

## Apprenticeship

Who agreed to teach you, sponsor you, test you, or send you where your training can continue?

## Debt or service

What obligation must you work off, honor, renegotiate, or escape without pretending it never mattered?

## Route work

What route needs a guide, pilot, scout, translator, porter, clerk, repair hand, or someone who knows the risks?

## Escort or convoy work

Who or what are you helping move safely: people, food, timber, water, medicine, records, tools, or trade goods?

## Recovery work after eruption damage

What rebuilding, survey, repair, relocation, archive work, or family recovery effort still reaches back to the eruption 60 years ago?

## Curiosity or scholarship

What question, craft, old record, natural pattern, language, route, or tradition are you trying to understand?

## Exile or self-exile

Who decided you should leave, or what made staying feel impossible for now?

## Religious question

What teaching, omen, vow, shrine practice, pilgrimage story, or disagreement with your elders needs lived experience rather than argument?

## Caldera-related fear, loss, or warning

What caution, loss, family rule, witnessed danger, or old warning makes the Caldera matter to you without giving you secret knowledge?

Once you have a travel reason, send your character concept to the DM. The group’s shared starting frame will be built from the submitted characters.
