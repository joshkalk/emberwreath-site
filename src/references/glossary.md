# Glossary

Quick definitions for common Emberwreath terms. Entries are short, player-facing, and meant to help you read character, atlas, and belief pages without digging for lore.

## A

**Ashspine:** [Kelden]({{ '/species/kelden/' | url }}) homeland of volcanic cliffs, ash slopes, vents, ledges, mining, rescue culture, and dangerous extraction.

**Aumari:** Soft-bodied brine-gel people from the [Tidefall Atolls]({{ '/atlas/island-quick-reference/' | url }}#tidefall-atolls), shaped by reefs, brine pools, tide roads, and fragile freshwater.

**Axali:** Freshwater amphibious people from [Hollowdeep]({{ '/atlas/island-quick-reference/' | url }}#hollowdeep), known for grottoes, hidden rivers, pigments, textiles, hospitality, and controlled access to water.

## B

**Breadbasket:** [Human]({{ '/species/humans/' | url }}) homeland and agricultural core of [Emberwreath]({{ '/start-here/what-is-emberwreath/' | url }}), known for farms, roads, granaries, mills, ports, crowns, and food politics.

**Breakwater:** Major cosmopolitan harbor, repair yard, warehouse island, and trade hub. It is powerful because goods and ships pass through it, but it does not rule Emberwreath.

**The Broken Wreath:** An older poetic and religious name for Emberwreath, often used when speaking about danger, fracture, memory, or sacred loss.

## C

**Caelmere:** [Scolen]({{ '/species/scolen/' | url }}) homeland of crater lakes, reservoirs, canals, archives, public works, water law, and civic memory.

**Caldera:** The dangerous volcanic heart of Emberwreath. Most people know it through warnings, route taboos, eruption memory, sailor stories, and fear of forbidden shortcuts.

**Cinderreach:** Nearly uninhabitable southeastern desert island of salt, cinder, heat glare, and failed survival.

**Civic-Harvest Covenant:** Human tradition of Breadbasket centered on food, roads, granaries, fair measures, public obligation, and the sacred duty to keep food moving.

## D

**Descent of Held Water:** Axali tradition of Hollowdeep centered on hidden water, wells, underground storage, ancestor witnesses, and the duties of drought memory.

**Discipline of Bloom:** Zivari tradition of Saffra centered on cultivation, beauty, medicine, dyes, spices, pruning, patience, and inherited craft.

## E

**Emberwreath:** A volcanic archipelago of homeland islands, trade routes, sacred waters, frontier settlements, dangerous seas, and old warnings. Sailors often call it the Wreath.

## F

**Ferren:** Wiry mustelid people from [Greenwood]({{ '/atlas/island-quick-reference/' | url }}#greenwood), shaped by cold forests, timber roads, harbors, ropeways, hunting, rescue, hearth, and kinship.

**Frozenfang:** Harsh seasonal northern mining frontier with Ferren-led summer camps and reused work sites. It is not a normal year-round homeland.

## G

**Greenwood:** Ferren homeland of cold forests, timber roads, harbors, ropeways, holds, old groves, and northern frontier life.

## H

**Hidden Continuity:** Thryss tradition of Veilgarden centered on preservation through concealment, fog, hidden valleys, protected routes, rare herbs, memory, and fragile continuities.

**Hollowdeep:** Axali homeland of grottoes, baths, pigments, textiles, hidden rivers, wells, sinkholes, and controlled water access.

**Homeland island:** An island where one playable species developed mostly separately before wider contact. A character can come from somewhere else, but homelands still matter culturally.

**Humans:** The most numerous people of Emberwreath, rooted in Breadbasket's farms, roads, granaries, ports, crowns, institutions, food systems, and political obligations.

## K

**Kelden:** Basalt-bodied people from Ashspine, shaped by volcanic cliffs, ash slopes, vents, ledges, rescue work, stone judgment, controlled risk, and calm strength.

## L

**Listening Tide:** Aumari tradition of Tidefall centered on tidal animism, negotiated coexistence, lagoons, tide pools, channels, pearl beds, freshwater lenses, and storm signs.

## M

**Measured Flow:** Scolen tradition of Caelmere centered on water guidance, reservoirs, canals, locks, measuring, public maintenance, and water duty.

**Mercy of the Boundary:** Veyr tradition of Sunspire centered on route guidance, boundary markers, warning, restraint, reservoirs, herding roads, and protection from places that should not be reopened.

## P

**Pilgrimage of the Unreturned Path:** Newer multi-species pilgrimage tradition of the [Veiled Reefs]({{ '/atlas/island-quick-reference/' | url }}#veiled-reefs), tied to hidden passages, safe return, thresholds, drowned memory, taboo lagoons, wrecks, sacred silence, and journeys where turning back matters.

## R

**Rainwall:** Small but vital water-export island of storms, cliffs, reservoirs, cisterns, dangerous harbors, and water-loading crews.

**Redwind:** Dry range frontier of salt, herding, wind, caravan roads, water officers, grazing compacts, and port transfer work.

**Reedmere:** Swampy textile and fiber island of reed beds, mangroves, rope, nets, sailcloth, sacks, dye yards, wetland processing, and export labor.

## S

**Saffra:** [Zivari]({{ '/species/zivari/' | url }}) homeland of warm terraces, orchards, medicines, dyes, spices, public reputation, and careful cultivation.

**Scolen:** Many-legged civic water people from Caelmere, shaped by reservoirs, canals, archives, water law, maintenance, and communal memory.

**Stormhook:** Maritime island of fishing, pilotage, rescue, lighthouses, current knowledge, and dangerous-sea expertise.

**Sunspire:** [Veyr]({{ '/species/veyr/' | url }}) homeland of dry routes, boundary markers, reservoirs, herding lands, new coastal trade life, and forbidden ruins.

## T

**Thryss:** Fog-fed plant-bodied people from [Veilgarden]({{ '/atlas/island-quick-reference/' | url }}#veilgarden), shaped by mist valleys, root terraces, damp stone, careful agriculture, elder memory, and patient stewardship.

**Tidefall Atolls:** Aumari homeland of reef flats, brine pools, tide roads, lagoons, pearl beds, fragile freshwater, and shallow-water travel.

**Twin Law of Ember and Stone:** Kelden tradition of Ashspine centered on the balance between heat, change, hunger, and revelation on one side, and shelter, endurance, boundary, and memory on the other.

## V

**Veiled Reefs:** Sacred reef region of pilgrimage waters, taboo lagoons, hidden channels, shrine islets, wreck memory, and ritual access. It is not a normal island homeland.

**Veilgarden:** Thryss homeland of fog valleys, root terraces, damp stone, hidden basins, rare crops, guarded knowledge, and careful agriculture.

**Verdurain:** Wet hostile jungle frontier with dangerous interior, limited camps, disease, flooding, failed settlements, and difficult mapping.

**Veyr:** Kindled people of Sunspire, made of mask, cord, clay, leather, and inner light, known as road-witnesses, route guides, boundary keepers, and protectors of dangerous warnings.

## W

**Wardens of Root and Name:** Ferren tradition of Greenwood centered on forest-ancestor wardship, named groves, hunting paths, root-sites, memory, and obligations between the dead, living, and unborn.

**Western Forbidden Caldera Sea:** Forbidden volcanic sea at Emberwreath's center, known for deadly shortcut temptation, volcanic danger, obsidian, toxic waters, ruin rumors, and sacred fear.

**The Wreath:** Common sailor shorthand for Emberwreath.

## Z

**Zivari:** Sharp-eyed people from Saffra, shaped by terraces, public display, productive proof, careful judgment, color, movement, reputation, and cultivation.
