# History & Beliefs

These pages give you the quick layer of Emberwreath's past and religious traditions: enough to shape a character, understand common references, and decide what your character was taught at home.

They are optional support for character creation, not homework. You can make a character with the [Make a Character]({{ '/make-a-character/' | url }}) path and return here only when you want more context.
